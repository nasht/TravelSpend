Travel Spend (BETA)

You're on the road and you want to grab a meal. But you're not sure if that 10,000 Bhutan Ngultrum or Eritrean Nakfa is going to break your budget? No problem.

Grab Travel Spend and track your daily, weekly or monthly budget and never wonder if you'll make it to the end of your journey. Supporting just about every currency on the planet, Travel Spend is an indispensable tool for globetrotters.

Simply add your budget, add your dates and track your outgoings to have a clear view of how much you've spent, and how much you can spend each day.

New: Easily organise your spend using unlimited, custom colour categories (tags).
